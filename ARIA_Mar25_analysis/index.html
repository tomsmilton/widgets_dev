Caveats

  • Values include amendments — contract values shown are the current total after amendments (extensions, scope increases). Amended contracts are marked with * and hovering shows the original award value. Amendment-only records (no matching original) are labelled (amendment)
  • Amendment matching is imperfect — 16 of 27 amendments could not be matched to their original contract due to name/context differences in the source data. These appear as separate amendment-only entries
  • Registered address used — supplier locations are based on Companies House registered address, which may differ from where work is performed
  • Some contracts unlocated — some contracts have suppliers with truncated or unresolved names from PDF extraction and are excluded from regional totals
  • Spend categories are estimated — the R&D / Activation / Operating split is inferred from contract context fields, not from ARIA's own classification
  • Financial transactions not shown — the 910 payment transactions in the database are separate from contract awards and not included here
