Every fixture here accepts a wired DMX signal — the same control line behind the Better Wakelight. Sorted cheap to cinema-scale, from a £130-ish RGB panel to a five-figure ARRI. Filter by type, colour, price, and — crucially for a build like the wakelight — whether the DMX port is a real 5-pin XLR or needs a 3.5 mm adapter.

A note on what's not here. Popular budget lights like the amaran 150c/300c and amaran COB S series are left out on purpose: they have no native wired DMX and only reach DMX through Aputure's separate Sidus bridge. For a board that drives a 5-pin XLR directly, the "Native XLR" rows are the ones that behave like the wakelight's Neewer panel.
